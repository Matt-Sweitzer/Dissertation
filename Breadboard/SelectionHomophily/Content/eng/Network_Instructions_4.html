Network Tutorial (Phase 3)

Next, some participants each round will have the opportunity to make a choice about a relationship with another participant. If this decision concerns someone currently in your network, you can choose between maintaining this relationship, or dissolving it. If you maintain the relationship, you will continue to discuss your opinions with this person. If you dissolve the relationship, you will cease communicating with them. If, on the other hand, your decision concerns someone not currently in your network, you can decide between adding this new relationship, or ignoring it. Again, the former choice will result in further communication with this person, while the latter choice will result in no further communication. You can have as many or as few people in your social network as you would like.
